Paris - The City of Light


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, and culture. Home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, it attracts millions of visitors annually.

  1. Eiffel Tower - A must-visit landmark, offering panoramic views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum - Famous for housing the Mona Lisa and thousands of other art pieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - A famous avenue known for shopping and theaters.
  5. Montmartre - The historic and artistic hilltop district.
  • Croissant - A flaky, buttery pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette - A long, thin loaf of French bread.
  • Escargot - Snails served with garlic butter, a classic French dish.
  • Macarons - Colorful, delicate cookies filled with creamy filling.
  • Coq au Vin - A traditional French chicken dish cooked in red wine.
